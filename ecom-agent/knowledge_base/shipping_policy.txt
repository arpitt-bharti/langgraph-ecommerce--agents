Shipping Policy

Orders are usually processed within 1-2 business days.

Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days depending on the destination.

Tracking information is generated once the order is shipped.

Delays may occur due to weather conditions, courier issues, public holidays, or incorrect shipping information.

Customers can track shipped orders using the tracking number provided in the order details.

If a shipment is delayed by more than 10 business days beyond the estimated delivery date, customers may contact support for investigation.
