Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 20 calendar days of the order date.

Eligibility Requirements:

* The order must have a successful payment status.
* The product must have been delivered.
* The item must be returned in its original condition unless it was damaged or defective upon arrival.

Refund requests are eligible in the following cases:

* Wrong item delivered.
* Damaged product received.
* Product received with missing components.
* Product significantly different from the description.
* Order delayed beyond 10 business days after the estimated delivery date.

Refund requests may be rejected if:

* More than 20 days have passed since the order date.
* The item shows signs of misuse, alteration, or accidental damage caused by the customer.
* The product belongs to a non-refundable category.
* The customer cannot provide sufficient information regarding the issue.

Refund Processing:

* Once approved, refunds are initiated to the original payment method.
* Credit Card refunds typically take 5-7 business days.
* Debit Card refunds typically take 5-10 business days.
* Net Banking refunds typically take 3-7 business days.
* UPI refunds typically take 1-3 business days.

Refund Status Definitions:

* Not Requested: No refund request has been submitted.
* Requested: Refund request has been received and is under review.
* Approved: Refund request has been approved and is awaiting processing.
* Completed: Refund has been successfully processed.
* Rejected: Refund request did not meet eligibility requirements.

Customers may contact support for clarification if a refund request is rejected.
